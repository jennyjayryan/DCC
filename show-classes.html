Show Classes

Classes contested at Dominion Chihuahua Club championship and open shows, as defined under Dogs New Zealand rules. Class eligibility is assessed on the day of the show.

Class Descriptions

NZ Kennel Club Show Classes

Dogs and bitches are shown separately in all classes. “a” suffix denotes the bitch class (e.g. Class 1 = dogs, Class 1a = bitches).

1 & 1a
Baby Puppy
Open to dogs and bitches aged 3 months and under 6 months on the day of the show. Baby Puppies compete for Best Baby Puppy in Show and are not eligible for Best in Show.
2 & 2a
Minor Puppy
Open to dogs and bitches aged 6 months and under 9 months on the day of the show.
3 & 3a
Puppy
Open to dogs and bitches aged 6 months and under 12 months on the day of the show.
4 & 4a
Novice
Open to dogs and bitches that have not won a first prize in any class other than Baby Puppy at a sanctioned show. Dogs that have won a Challenge Certificate are not eligible for Novice.
5 & 5a
Junior
Open to dogs and bitches aged 12 months and under 24 months on the day of the show.
6 & 6a
Intermediate
Open to dogs and bitches aged 24 months and under 36 months on the day of the show.
7 & 7a
Limit
Open to dogs and bitches that have won no more than 4 Challenge Certificates. Overseas champions are not eligible. Dogs holding New Zealand champion status are not eligible.
8 & 8a
New Zealand Bred
Open to dogs and bitches whelped in New Zealand that are 6 months of age or over on the day of the show. Both parents must be registered with Dogs New Zealand.
9 & 9a
Stud Dog & Brood Bitch
Open to breeding animals who have progeny registered with Dogs New Zealand from at least one litter. The sire or dam must be shown with a minimum of two of their progeny.
10 & 10a
Veteran
Open to dogs and bitches aged 7 years or over on the day of the show. Veterans compete for Best Veteran in Show and may also be eligible for Best in Show.
11 & 11a
Open
Open to any dog or bitch aged 6 months or over on the day of the show, regardless of any prior wins or titles. This is the prestige class — all breeds champions are welcome.
18 & 18a
Neutered
Open to neutered dogs and bitches aged 6 months or over on the day of the show. Exhibits compete for Best Neuter in Show; they are not eligible for Best in Show or Challenge Certificates.

Championship Shows only: Challenge Certificates and Reserve Challenge Certificates are awarded at Championship Shows. A dog that wins 3 Challenge Certificates under at least 3 different judges, including one as an adult, is entitled to the title New Zealand Champion (NZ CH). Open Shows award points toward a Dog Show Title only.
