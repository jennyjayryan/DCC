Official Playgroup Partner

Thank You, Kuri

Kuri are the proud sponsors of our weekly Saturday Playgroup — providing not only their generous support, but the very building where our Chihuahuas gather every week. Without Kuri, there would be no playgroup.

Without Kuri’s incredible generosity — their space, their support, and their genuine love of dogs — our weekly playgroup simply would not exist. They are far more than a sponsor; they are the heart of our community.
Our Partnership

The Home of Our Playgroup

Every Saturday from 10:00 am to 11:30 am, the Dominion Chihuahua Club gathers at Kuri’s facility in Canterbury for our beloved weekly playgroup. Kuri generously make their building available to us, giving our members and their Chihuahuas a safe, warm, and welcoming space to socialise and play.

This partnership is something we treasure deeply. Running a regular playgroup requires a reliable venue, and Kuri provide that — week after week — entirely out of their commitment to the wellbeing of dogs and the communities that love them.

We encourage every DCC member and visitor to support Kuri in return. When you need dog care services in Canterbury, Kuri should be your first call.

What Kuri Offer

Dog Care You Can Trust

Kuri are dedicated to the health, happiness, and wellbeing of dogs across New Zealand. Their team of passionate dog lovers offer a range of professional services designed to give your dog the very best.

Boarding & Daycare

Safe, comfortable boarding and daycare so your dog is happy and cared for while you’re away. Kuri’s facilities are purpose-built for dog wellbeing.

Grooming
