Our Story

About the Club

Founded in 1964 in Canterbury, New Zealand, the Dominion Chihuahua Club Inc is one of the country’s most respected breed clubs — dedicated to promoting and protecting the beautiful Chihuahua.

For over six decades, our club has brought together Chihuahua enthusiasts from across Canterbury and beyond. We celebrate the breed’s unique spirit through education, responsible breeding, and — most of all — our beloved weekly playgroups.

The Breed

Chihuahua Breed Standard

Small, dainty, alert, and swift-moving — the Chihuahua is a big personality in a tiny, elegant package.

🔬

Compact Size

Under 3 kg — the world’s smallest recognised breed, perfectly portable and full of life

👁️

Expressive Eyes

Large, luminous, round eyes set wide apart — full of intelligence and character

⚡

Swift & Alert

Quick, graceful movement with a confident, saucy expression and erect ears

🧠

Spirited Temperament

Bold, loyal, and devoted — a fierce and tender heart in every Chihuahua

Two Coat Varieties

Smooth Coat

Short, glossy, close-lying coat. Clean, sleek lines that showcase the breed’s elegant structure and definition.

Long Coat

Soft, silky, fine coat — slightly wavy or flat. A flowing mane, feathered ears, and a plumed tail complete the look.
