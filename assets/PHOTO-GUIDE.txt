DOMINION CHIHUAHUA CLUB – PHOTO & VIDEO GUIDE
==============================================

Drop your photos and videos in the folders below, then update
the matching elements in index.html and css/styles.css.

IMAGES  (place in assets/images/)
----------------------------------
hero.jpg            Hero banner – landscape, Chihuahuas at play, 1920×1080+
                    In styles.css → .hero__bg, set:
                    background-image: url('assets/images/hero.jpg');
                    background-size: cover; background-position: center;

smooth-coat.jpg     Profile/side shot of a smooth coat Chihuahua, high-res
long-coat.jpg       Front/profile of a long coat showing silky fur, high-res
                    In index.html replace the .coat-photo divs with:
                    <img src="assets/images/smooth-coat.jpg" alt="Smooth Coat Chihuahua" />

playgroup-1.jpg     Main playgroup shot (bento hero cell)
playgroup-2.jpg     Dogs socialising
playgroup-3.jpg     Playtime / action shot
playgroup-4.jpg     Group / wide shot
playgroup-5.jpg     Zoomies / running shot
                    In index.html replace .bento-bg-* divs with:
                    <img src="assets/images/playgroup-1.jpg" alt="..." class="bento-img" />
                    Add to CSS: .bento-img { width:100%; height:100%; object-fit:cover; }

specialty-show.jpg  2026 Specialty Show or a ribbon/ring shot
past-event.jpg      2019 Speciality Show photo
                    In index.html replace the .event-photo divs with <img> tags.

VIDEOS  (place in assets/videos/)
----------------------------------
playgroup-highlight.mp4    Playgroup highlights reel
breed-showcase.mp4         Breed showcase video
                           Replace .video-thumb divs with:
                           <video src="assets/videos/playgroup-highlight.mp4" controls poster="assets/images/playgroup-1.jpg"></video>

TIPS
----
- Use WebP format for images where possible (smaller file size, great quality)
- Aim for < 500 KB per image after compression (TinyPNG or Squoosh are great tools)
- Keep originals backed up separately
- Minimum recommended size: 800×600 for cards, 1920×1080 for hero/bento
